Our Upcoming Events


Author’s Live Q&As

Each month, we host a live Q&A with an author. These events are held in independent bookshops and live streamed for our community.

March’s event: Join us at Shelf & Spine in Bristol for an evening with debut novelist Maya Okafor, discussing her critically acclaimed first novel The Cartographer’s Daughter. The event will be live streamed for members who can’t attend in person.

Book Launch Giveaways

Every month we partner with an independent publisher to give away five signed copies of a new release to our community members. To enter, simply register as a member and keep an eye on our monthly newsletter — winners are drawn at random and announced on the last Friday of each month.

This month’s giveaway: Five signed copies of Still Waters by R. J. Calloway, published by Bloodhound Books.

Monthly Reading Challenges

Our monthly reading challenge involves working your way through our Featured Reading List for that month.

Reading lists are sent to our mailing list via our monthly newsletter. You must register as a member to receive this to your inbox.

This month’s Featured Reading List:

  • The Thursday Murder Club – Richard Osman
  • Lessons in Chemistry – Bonnie Garmus
  • Tomorrow, and Tomorrow, and Tomorrow – Gabrielle Zevin
  • Intermezzo – Sally Rooney
  • James – Percival Everett
  • The Women – Kristin Hannah
