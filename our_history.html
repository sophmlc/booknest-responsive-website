The BookNest Story

Our Beginning

Launched in 2020, BookNest was created by book lovers during lockdown as a way to champion the independent bookshops that bring so much character and community to our high streets. What started as a small directory has grown into a national platform that connects readers with stores they might never have found otherwise.

Our Journey

In our first year we partnered with thirty stores across England, hosting online author events when in-person gatherings were impossible. Word spread quickly — readers were hungry for a way to stay connected to the books and booksellers they loved. By 2022 we had expanded to Scotland and Wales, and our monthly subscription box launched to a waiting list of over two thousand.

Today we work with stores from Cornwall to the Highlands, running live Q&A events, curating reading challenges, and sending BookNest boxes to members every month. Our mission remains what it has always been: to make it as easy as possible to choose independent.
